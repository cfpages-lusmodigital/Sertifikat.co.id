# Article Catalog — sertifikat.co.id

## Scope and publication rule

This catalog turns the 12-topic map into 72 non-geographic, non-overlapping briefs. `Sertifikat.co.id` is treated as an independent information, training, referral, and certification-facilitation property unless current primary evidence proves a narrower provider or authorization claim. Before publishing any commercial or named-entity claim, identify the legal provider, LSP/issuer, scheme, TUK, assessor role, contractual counterparty, privacy controller, license/accreditation scope and status, verification date, and source.

Priorities: P0 is the first corrective/foundation cluster; P1 builds the principal decision journeys; P2 completes operational coverage; P3 is supporting depth. Waves indicate editorial sequence, not a promise that an article is ready without evidence and qualified review.

## First-wave publication gate

Before any P0 brief is published, reconcile it with the existing URL inventory and correct unsupported authority language on the homepage, LSP pages, KADIN pages, structured data, and commercial templates. A publishable brief must cite current primary sources, show a verification date and responsible reviewer, state who actually performs each role, avoid all pass/recognition/employment/compliance guarantees, and pass qualified legal, accreditation, K3, privacy, or accessibility review where applicable.
